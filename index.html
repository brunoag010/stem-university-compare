⚙️ Methodology
Salary — College Scorecard md_earn_wne_p10: median earnings 10 years post-entry. Graduation rate — Scorecard C150_4: 6-year completion rate. Employment — split into empI (direct industry) and empG (graduate school enrollment). For science fields (Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Neuroscience, etc.) where grad school continuation rates exceed 28%, the tool treats empI + empG as the combined success outcome — so physics programs are not penalised for sending graduates to PhD programs. Debt — Scorecard DEBT_MDN: median cumulative federal loan debt at graduation. Default rate — Scorecard CDR3: 3-year cohort default rate. Cost of Attendance — Scorecard COSTT4_A (average net cost). Field salaries — BLS OOH 2024–25 median wages adjusted for university prestige tier. Value Score = salary ÷ COA, normalised 0–100.
📅 Data Currency
Scorecard: AY2023–24 release ↗ BLS wages: May 2023 OEWS ↗ Tuition/COA: AY2024–25 published rates ↗
